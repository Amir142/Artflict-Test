About Us
Artflict is a startup created by students in the MEET program (Middle East Entrepreneurs Of Tomorrow).
Our aim is to create a more compassionate and tolerant society where art is used more frequently as a means of expression.
We are trying to help people suffering from the Palestinian-Israeli conflict in their daily lives by allowing them to express themselves using words and visual art.
